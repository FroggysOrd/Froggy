Mission Statement

We Froggys are more than just pixels on a screen. We are a community of degens and dreamers, bound by the shared values of friendship, exploration, and collaborative growth. Our minimalistic 6x6-pixel sOrdinals inscriptions are the face of our digital journey.

Drawing inspiration from 'The Little Prince,' 'The Bitcoin White Paper,' and 'The Sovereign Individual', we learn from these inspiring texts, as well as from each other and our collective experience.

We strive to achieve financial freedom, but just like the little prince traversing the cosmos in search of companionship, we too seek genuine human connections.

We look for a place where risk-taking meets reflection, and innovation meets introspection. Here diversity is celebrated, and mistakes are embraced as opportunities for learning and growth. We cultivate an environment filled with collaboration, support, and open-mindedness.

By embracing the ethos of crypto degens and fearless curiosity, we encourage a culture unafraid of ridicule or errors, seeing them instead as valuable lessons in our collective journey of growth and understanding. We believe in the power of community, inviting members to connect with, support and uplift one another while sharing knowledge, experiences, and the occasional meme.

We foster connection, be it through the joy of making new friends, exploring the vast potential of Bitcoin and its L2s, or simply embracing the journey of self-discovery. Together, we embark on this noble quest, fueled by curiosity and courage.

Join us as we redefine the boundaries of creativity, collaboration, and friendship in the digital age. Together, let's free the child within and embark on an adventure unlike any other. HOP IN!
